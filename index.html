Wampus Wanderers

Welcome to Wampus Wanderers!

Our goal is to help students find the personalized perfect roommate and make finding housing easier

Made for UT students by UT students. We felt students' universal panic from having to scramble to curate next year's roommates and which apartment complex to live in just a few months into the semester.

Having to rigourously search online for which complex fits all your roommates' (if you even have that figured out!) needs while also dealing with UT's courseload is a harrowing task.
